Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our delivery service.

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our website under "Track Order" or directly on the courier's website. Express and Premium delivery customers will also receive SMS updates.

For Standard and Express deliveries, the courier will attempt to leave your package in a safe place or with a neighbor. If this isn't possible, they will leave a card with instructions to rearrange delivery or collect from a local depot. Premium delivery customers can select a specific time slot to ensure they're home.

Standard delivery is Monday to Friday only. Express delivery includes Saturday delivery for orders placed before 2pm on Friday. Premium delivery includes Saturday and Sunday options in select areas. Please check availability during checkout.

Yes, you can change your delivery address as long as your order hasn't been dispatched yet. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible with your order number and new delivery details. Once an order has been dispatched, we cannot change the delivery address.

Subscription customers receive free standard delivery on all orders. You can upgrade to Express or Premium delivery for an additional fee. Subscription deliveries are scheduled according to your chosen frequency, and you'll receive a notification email three days before each delivery is processed, giving you time to make any changes if needed.
