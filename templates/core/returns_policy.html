Returns Policy

At {{ site_settings.site_name }}, we take pride in the quality of our Welsh spring water products. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Returns Policy outlines our procedures for returns, exchanges, and refunds.

Return Eligibility

{{ settings.returns_policy }}

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging
  • Accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase

Return Timeframe

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. After 30 days, we cannot offer you a refund or exchange.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Products that have been opened or unsealed
  • Products that show signs of use or tampering
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable products
  • Personalized or custom-made items

Return Process

To return a product:

  1. Contact our customer service team at {{ site_settings.contact_email }} or {{ site_settings.contact_phone }} to obtain a Return Authorization (RA) number
  2. Include your RA number, order number, and reason for return in your package
  3. Ship your return to the address provided by our customer service team

Return Shipping

You are responsible for the cost of returning the product unless:

  • The product was damaged during shipping
  • You received an incorrect product
  • The product is defective

In these cases, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label and reimburse your original shipping costs.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days. Please note that it may take an additional 2-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in the following circumstances:

  • Products with obvious signs of use
  • Products that are returned more than 14 days after delivery
  • Products that are not in their original condition or packaging

Exchanges

If you need to exchange a product for a different size, variant, or product altogether, please follow the same process as for returns. Once we receive the original product, we will process your new order.

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damaged product and packaging
  • We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you

Subscription Products

For subscription products:

  • You may return any individual delivery within 30 days
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings
  • Refunds for subscription payments will be processed according to our standard refund policy

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Returns Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: {{ site_settings.contact_email }}
  • Phone: {{ site_settings.contact_phone }}

Need Help With Your Order?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any questions or concerns.
