Our Welsh Heritage

Discover the rich history and traditions behind Trederwen Springs, deeply rooted in Welsh culture and natural beauty.

A Legacy of Purity

For centuries, the Welsh mountains have been known for their pristine springs and the exceptional quality of their water. Our company continues this proud tradition, bringing the purest Welsh spring water to the modern world.

The name "Trederwen" comes from ancient Welsh, combining "tre" (home) and "derwen" (oak), symbolizing strength and longevity rooted in the Welsh landscape. Just as the mighty oak draws nourishment from deep within the earth, our springs tap into ancient aquifers beneath the Welsh mountains.

"The mountains of Wales have been the guardians of our water for millennia."

Our History

The journey of Trederwen Springs from a local secret to a premium water brand.

1895

Local shepherd Dafydd Williams discovers a natural spring with exceptional water while tending his flock in the Snowdonia mountains. The spring quickly becomes known for its healing properties among local villagers.

1920

The Williams family begins bottling the spring water in small batches for distribution to nearby towns, establishing the first iteration of what would become Trederwen Springs.

1952

Expansion to additional springs in the Brecon Beacons and Cambrian Mountains, with scientific analysis confirming the exceptional mineral content and purity of the water sources.

1985

Modern bottling facilities established with a commitment to environmental sustainability and preservation of the natural springs. The company officially adopts the name "Trederwen Springs."

2010

Introduction of eco-friendly packaging and carbon-neutral operations, reinforcing our commitment to protecting the Welsh natural environment that provides our exceptional water.

Today

Trederwen Springs is recognized globally for its premium Welsh spring water, while maintaining its deep connection to Welsh heritage and sustainable practices. We continue to innovate while honoring the traditions that have made our water special for generations.
