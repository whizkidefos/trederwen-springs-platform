Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions.

Standard shipping within the UK takes 2-4 business days. Express shipping is available for next-day delivery if ordered before 2 PM. International shipping times vary by destination, typically 5-10 business days for Europe and 7-14 business days for the rest of the world.

You can track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. Alternatively, you can use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email. We also offer real-time tracking updates via text message if you opt-in during checkout.

Welsh spring water is naturally filtered through ancient rock formations in the Welsh mountains, giving it a unique mineral profile and exceptional purity. Our water contains a balanced blend of essential minerals including calcium, magnesium, and silica, with low sodium content. The protected environment of our spring sources ensures that the water remains untouched by modern pollution.

Our bottled water has a shelf life of 2 years when stored properly. For best taste, we recommend consuming within 1 year of the bottling date. Store bottles in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and strong odors. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within 7 days for optimal freshness.

You can update your payment information by logging into your account, navigating to "Account Settings," and selecting "Payment Methods." From there, you can add new payment methods, update existing ones, or change your default payment method. All payment information is securely encrypted and stored in compliance with PCI DSS standards.

To change your subscription frequency, log into your account and go to "My Subscriptions." Select the subscription you wish to modify, then click "Edit Subscription." From there, you can adjust the delivery frequency, quantity, or products included. Changes made at least 48 hours before your next scheduled delivery will apply to that delivery; otherwise, they will take effect on the following delivery.

Yes, you can pause your subscription for up to 3 months or cancel it at any time. To do this, log into your account, go to "My Subscriptions," select the subscription, and click either "Pause Subscription" or "Cancel Subscription." If you pause, you'll be asked to select when you'd like deliveries to resume. There are no fees for pausing or canceling your subscription.
