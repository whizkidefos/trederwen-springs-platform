Our Mountain Story

Trederwen Springs was born from a deep appreciation for the pristine waters that flow through the ancient Welsh mountains. For generations, locals have known about the exceptional purity of these springs, naturally filtered through layers of rock formed over millennia.

Founded in 1998 by the Davies family, our company began with a simple mission: to share the gift of Welsh mountain water with the world while preserving the natural heritage of our valleys. What started as a small local operation has grown into a beloved brand that stays true to its Welsh roots.

Today, we continue to source our water from protected springs nestled in the heart of Wales, ensuring each drop carries the essence of our mountains while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship.

Our Mountain Values

The principles that guide us are as steadfast as the mountains from which our water flows.

Natural Purity

We believe in preserving the natural purity of our water. Our springs are carefully selected for their exceptional quality, and we maintain minimal processing to deliver water just as nature intended.

Welsh Heritage

Our Welsh roots run deep. We honor the traditions and culture of Wales in everything we do, from our sustainable practices to supporting local communities in the valleys we call home.

Sustainability

We are committed to protecting the environment that gives us our precious water. From responsible sourcing to eco-friendly packaging, sustainability guides every decision we make.

Our Mountain Journey

The path from Welsh springs to becoming Wales' premium water brand.

1998

The First Drop

Trederwen Springs was founded by the Davies family, who discovered an exceptional spring on their ancestral land in North Wales. The first bottles were sold at local farmers' markets.

2005

Growing Streams

After winning several taste awards, Trederwen expanded distribution across Wales and into major UK retailers, while maintaining our commitment to sustainable practices.

2012

Valley Innovation

We introduced our innovative eco-friendly packaging and launched our first flavored water varieties, infused with Welsh-grown botanicals.

2018

Mountain Heritage

Celebrating our 20th anniversary, we established the Trederwen Mountain Conservation Trust to protect Welsh watersheds and launched our subscription service.

Today

Flowing Forward

Trederwen Springs continues to grow while staying true to our Welsh roots. We're proud to bring the purity of Welsh mountain water to customers worldwide while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and community.

Our Sustainability Pledge

At Trederwen Springs, we believe that the purity of our water is directly linked to the health of our environment. That's why sustainability isn't just a buzzword for us—it's at the core of everything we do.

100% Recyclable Packaging

All our bottles and packaging materials are fully recyclable, and we're working toward using 100% recycled materials by 2026.

Carbon Neutral Operations

We offset 100% of our carbon emissions and are investing in renewable energy to power our facilities.

Watershed Protection

5% of our profits go to the Trederwen Mountain Conservation Trust, protecting Welsh watersheds for future generations.
