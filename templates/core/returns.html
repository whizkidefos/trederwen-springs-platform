Returns Policy

Our hassle-free returns process ensures your complete satisfaction with every purchase.

Our Returns Promise

At Trederwen Springs, we stand behind the quality of our premium Welsh spring water products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a simple and straightforward returns process.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most products, provided they are in their original condition and packaging. For water products specifically, we can only accept returns if the products are unopened and the seals remain intact.

Our Quality Guarantee

If you receive any damaged or defective products, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

"Your satisfaction is our highest priority."

Return Policy Details

Important information about our returns policy.

Eligible Items

Most products are eligible for return within 30 days of delivery. Water products must be unopened with intact seals. Personalized items and gift cards are not eligible for return unless defective.

Return Shipping Costs

For standard returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. For damaged or defective items, we'll provide a prepaid return label or arrange collection at no cost to you.

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase. If the original payment method is unavailable, we'll issue a store credit or process the refund via an alternative method agreed upon with the customer.

Subscription Products

Subscription customers enjoy an extended 45-day return window and free return shipping on all orders. Please contact our customer service team directly to process subscription returns.

Exceptions

Special promotions, clearance items, or products marked as "final sale" may have different return policies, which will be clearly indicated at the time of purchase.
