Our Sustainability Goals

We've set ambitious targets to minimize our environmental impact and maximize our positive contribution.

Carbon Neutrality

We've reduced our carbon emissions by 90% and offset the remainder through local reforestation projects.

Target: 100% by 2025

Sustainable Packaging

75% of our packaging is now made from recycled or plant-based materials that are fully recyclable.

Target: 100% by 2026

Water Conservation

We've reduced water usage in our operations by 70% through innovative recycling and conservation systems.

Target: 85% by 2027

Sustainability Initiatives

Discover the concrete actions we're taking to protect our environment and communities.

100% Renewable Energy

Our facilities are powered entirely by renewable energy sources, including our own hydroelectric generators and solar panels installed on all our buildings.

Reduced CO2 emissions by 2,500 tons annually

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We've pioneered the use of plant-based PET bottles and recycled materials for all our packaging, significantly reducing plastic waste.

Eliminated 200 tons of virgin plastic annually

Watershed Protection

We've established protected zones around all our spring sources, working with local communities and conservation groups to maintain the natural ecosystem.

Protected over 5,000 acres of Welsh wilderness

Community Sustainability

We support local sustainability initiatives through our community grant program, empowering Welsh communities to develop their own environmental projects.

Funded 35 community projects since 2018
