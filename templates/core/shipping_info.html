Shipping Policy

At {{ site_settings.site_name }}, we strive to provide reliable and efficient delivery services for our premium Welsh spring water products. This shipping policy outlines our delivery process, timeframes, and other important information.

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all addresses within the United Kingdom, including England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. For international shipping inquiries, please contact our customer service team.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing may take up to 3 business days.

Once your order is processed, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information where applicable.

Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery timeframes are as follows:

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days
  • Next Day Delivery: Next business day (for orders placed before 2pm Monday-Friday)

Please note that these timeframes are estimates and not guarantees. Delivery times may be affected by factors outside our control, such as weather conditions, public holidays, or carrier delays.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on your selected delivery method and order value:

  • Standard Delivery: £3.99 (Free for orders over £25)
  • Express Delivery: £5.99 (Free for orders over £50)
  • Next Day Delivery: £7.99 (Free for orders over £75)

Shipping costs will be displayed at checkout before payment is required.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information where applicable. You can also track your order by:

  • Logging into your account and viewing your order history
  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contacting our customer service team with your order number

Delivery Instructions

You can provide specific delivery instructions during checkout. If you have special requirements for your delivery, please include them in the "Delivery Instructions" field.

Failed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt is unsuccessful:

  • The carrier will leave a card with instructions on how to arrange redelivery or collection
  • The package will typically be held at the local depot for up to 7 days
  • After this period, undelivered packages will be returned to us

If your package is returned to us due to a failed delivery, we will contact you to arrange redelivery. Additional shipping charges may apply.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your package arrives damaged or items are missing:

  • Inspect all packages upon delivery and note any damage on the delivery receipt
  • Take photos of any damaged packaging or products
  • Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery

We will investigate all claims and arrange for replacement products or refunds as appropriate.

Subscription Deliveries

For our Valley Delivery subscription service:

  • Deliveries are made according to your selected frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly)
  • You can pause, skip, or modify your delivery schedule through your account
  • Changes to upcoming deliveries must be made at least 48 hours before the scheduled processing date
